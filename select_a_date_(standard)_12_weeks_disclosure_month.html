Choose a reading room

Choose a reading room

If you are unsure of which room you need, choose this one.

You can use this room if any of the documents you are ordering are maps, large documents or dated pre-16th century.

We can change room allocation later if we need to.

Need a camera stand?

All seats in the Document reading room have a camera stand provided. If you are visiting the Map and large document reading room, tell us if you need a camera stand in the ‘Access needs and research requests’ box when you order your documents in step 4.

Are accessible seats available?

All seats in our reading rooms are accessible. You can tell us about your other access needs when you order your documents in step 4.
