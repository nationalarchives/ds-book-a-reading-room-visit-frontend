Tell us which documents you would like to see

You have a provisional reservation for a visit to the reading rooms. Complete this form to order original documents held by The National Archives.

If you do not complete this step by Monday 29 March 2021 at 11:59 PM British Summer Time (BST) your visit will be automatically cancelled.

If you provided an email address as part of your booking, a direct link to this form is also in your reservation confirmation email, so you can return to edit or cancel your document order at any point before Monday 29 March 2021 at 11:59 PM British Summer Time (BST). If you cannot see the reservation confirmation email in your inbox, check your spam and junk email folders.

If you did not provide an email address as part of your booking, you can return to your booking before the deadline at this link:
https://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/book-a-reading-room-visit/return-to-booking

Once this date has passed, you cannot change your document order as we will begin preparing your order for your visit.

Enter up to 12 full catalogue references

Documents can be from multiple series, for example ADM 22 and C 142.
Search our catalogue to find catalogue references.

You need to add at least 1 more document before the deadline to avoid your order being cancelled.

Enter up to 3 reserve catalogue references

You can provide 3 reserve catalogue references in case your first choices are unavailable.

Tell us about your access needs, including if you have mobility access requirements. You can also use this space to tell us your research requests, including library materials, microfilm/microfiche, or finding/preservation aids that are normally available on self-service.

We cannot guarantee that all of the research materials you request will be available.
